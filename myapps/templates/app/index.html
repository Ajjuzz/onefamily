Eligiblity
  • 1. The donar's age should be 18 to 60 years.
  • 2. Donar should not be suffering, or had history of cancer.
  • 3. Donar should not be suffering from any blood clotting disorder, such as haemophilia.
  • 4. Donar should not be undertaking any kind of treatments
  • 5. If any more doubts visit blood bank

Facts about Blood
  • 1. In human body Blood makes up about 7 percent of our body's weight.
  • 2. There are four types of transfusable products that can be derived from blood: red cells, platelets, plasma and cryoprecipitate.
  • 3. Donated platelets must be used within five days of collection.
  • 4. Donors can give either whole blood or specific blood components only. The process of donating specific blood components – red cells, plasma or platelets – is called apheresis.
Facts about Donars
  • 1. One donation can help save the lives of up to three people.
  • 2. A healthy donor may donate red blood cells every 56 days, or double red cells every 112 days.
  • 3. A healthy donor may donate platelets as few as 7 days apart, but a maximum of 24 times a year.
